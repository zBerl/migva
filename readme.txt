 Welcome to the Mental Illness and Gun Violence Awareness Charity Project Winter 2023 Website (CIS-257A-W01X)

Explore the website to learn more about the organization and its initiatives:

Home: The homepage offers an overview of the organization, featuring an animated logo and a slideshow created with Bootstrap. The slideshow includes a video link on the first slide and two image links on slides 2 and 3, connecting you to other sections of the site. Our Mission and logo, both designed as SVG files using Affinity Designer, to help ensure faster load times.

About: Discover more about our mission in this section, featuring an engaging layout with an image complementing the text and effectively conveying our values.

Events: Stay up-to-date with upcoming events and activities in support of our cause. The "Learn More" buttons navigate to a quick overview table displaying event dates and times.

Initiatives: Learn about our various programs and initiatives focused on mental illness and gun violence awareness. The images used in this section are sourced from Pixabay, a free and open-source platform, comments have been provided in the HTML for a direct link to the source of those files.

Contact: Fill out the contact form provided in this section to get in touch with us. Discussion: Connect with our community through our linked Twitter feed.

Donate: Designed to maintain focus on the donation process, this form is supported by Bootstrap 4.0 the code is accessible at the base of each HTML file.
